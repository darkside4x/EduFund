About EduFund

EduFund is a revolutionary platform that leverages blockchain technology to facilitate transparent and secure donations for educational purposes. Our mission is to create a decentralized ecosystem where donors can directly contribute to students' educational needs.

Using Ethereum smart contracts, we ensure that every donation is traceable and reaches its intended beneficiary, making the process of educational funding more efficient and trustworthy.

Built for PEC Hacks

This project was developed during PEC Hacks, one of India's premier hackathons focusing on blockchain innovation and social impact. Our team aimed to address the critical challenge of educational funding accessibility through decentralized technology.
